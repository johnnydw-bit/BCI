The opportunity

Members have ideas.
We needed a better way to hear them.

📬

Ideas were scattered

Suggestions arrived by email, at the bar, in passing conversation — no consistent record.

⚖️

No fair scoring

Without a structured process, louder voices could outweigh better ideas.

📭

Members in the dark

Members rarely heard back about what happened to their suggestions.

A key benefit

Stronger engagement between members and management

🗣️

Members feel heard

Every idea is acknowledged. Members receive a personal AI assessment within seconds of submitting — not silence.

🔄

Closed feedback loop

When a decision is made, the member is told — with a reason. They can see the status of every idea they've raised at any time.

📈

Evidence of responsiveness

The Board can point to implemented ideas, in-progress ideas, and the volume of member engagement as concrete evidence of an open, responsive club.

💬 Members who feel listened to are more likely to renew, recommend the club to others, and engage positively with the club's direction.
The solution

A purpose-built improvement portal

📱

Member-facing portal

Members submit ideas from any device. The portal installs as an app on phones — no app store needed.

🤖

AI-assisted scoring

Every idea is automatically scored across six dimensions: impact, feasibility, cost, novelty, strategic fit, and experience.

📋

Board triage dashboard

Directors see a prioritised, scored list of ideas. Filter by area, owner, flags, or Board member submissions.

✉️

Automatic member updates

Members receive personalised email notifications when their idea is assessed or its status changes.

Member experience

Submitting an idea takes two minutes

1

Sign in with name & email

Members access the portal via a link on the club website. No passwords — just name and email.

2

Describe the improvement

What they'd like to see changed, why it would benefit members, which area it relates to, and how many members it affects.

3

Instant AI feedback

Within seconds, an AI preliminary assessment is shown — plain English, no jargon. Full scoring runs overnight.

4

Track progress in My Improvements

Members can log back in at any time to see the status of every idea they've submitted.

Quality control

Every submission passes an automatic moderation gate

🚫

Inappropriate content

Offensive language, personal complaints, or content unrelated to the club.

🔁

Duplicates

Near-identical ideas from the same member are flagged and the member is invited to resubmit with added context.

🌫️

Vague or unactionable

"Make the course better" is rejected. The system explains why and encourages a specific resubmission.

Rejected submissions are logged for the Club Manager to review — nothing is silently discarded without a record.
Assessment engine

Six-dimension AI scoring

👥

Member impact

How many members benefit, and how much does it improve their experience?

🎯

Strategic alignment

How well does it fit the club's current priorities and direction?

🔧

Feasibility

How realistic is implementation? Are there constraints or dependencies?

💰

Cost vs benefit

Estimated cost range and confidence, weighed against likely benefit delivered.

💡

Novelty

Is this a genuinely fresh idea or standard practice at clubs of this level?

⭐

Prioritisation

Score bands tell the Board what to focus on

Band What it means Action
Priority High score — strong case across all dimensions Passed to relevant director immediately
Active queue Good score — worthy of the next review cycle Reviewed at next Board cycle
Holding Solid idea, longer timeframe Recorded for future consideration
Low priority Noted but unlikely to be progressed soon Remains visible; member notified
Already in plan Great minds think alike Member told their idea is in progress
⚡ Quick wins — ideas that can be implemented in under 4 weeks at under £500 — are flagged and can be actioned ahead of the standard cycle regardless of score band.

Intelligent features

Built-in safety and pattern detection

⚠️

Health & Safety flagging

Any submission with a safety dimension is automatically flagged in red and surfaced to the top of the triage list, regardless of its overall score.

🔗

Cluster detection

When multiple members submit similar ideas independently, they are grouped into a cluster. Clustered ideas receive a consensus bonus — the Board can see recurring themes at a glance.

📅

Seasonal windows

Ideas tied to a specific time of year (e.g. winter drainage, summer catering) are flagged with the optimal implementation window.

💼

Suggested ownership

The AI recommends which director should own each idea based on category and content — reducing triage time significantly.

Governance & privacy

Fair, transparent, and data-responsible

✓

All ideas treated equally

Scoring is AI-driven and consistent — no idea is advanced or suppressed based on who submitted it.

✓

Anonymous submissions respected

Members who choose anonymous recognition are not identified to directors in the triage view.

✓

Data stored securely

All data is stored on encrypted cloud infrastructure. Email addresses are used only for programme communications and never shared.

✓

Full audit trail

Every status change is logged with a timestamp. Members can see the complete history of their submissions.
